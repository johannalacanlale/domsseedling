MEET THE SEEDLING FAMILY!

Kris Blake

Kris Blake, along with her incredibly support husband Thad, is the owner and founder of Seedling Theatre Company. She has been continuously been involved in one stage production or another for the past 8 years and has worn just about every hat in the theatre prop closet. Kris graduated from Brigham Young University in 1996 with a degree in Youth Agency Adminstration and has worked with children and teens through numerous organizations non-stop since highschool. She renewed her high school love for the performing arts when her daughter, at a very young age, jumped on stage and never came down. Kris has worked as a children's musical theatre director, costumer designer, set designer, acting coach and director of a performing arts preschool program. She most recently has taught Kinder-Prep at American Heritage Academy where she also wrote and directed the school's theatre productions. In 2015, Kris created and directed an interactive community theatre group, Staged Right, focused on serving special populations, especially the Autism Community with which she has a personal connection.

Britni Favero

Britni Favero is the owner and teacher at Born to Sing Vocals, a private voice lessons company. She has been studying and teaching the IVA technique (Institute for Vocal Advancement) for the past 11 years and is a level 3 instructor. She has studied with the acclaimed Jeffrey Skousen as both a vocal student and teacher and is continually adding to her skills and knowledge of voice pedagogy, vocal science, and performance development. In her own words: “I LOVE what I do. I love teaching. I love the voice. I love helping others connect and strengthen voices they’ve had all along or voices they never even knew were hidden there. This isn’t a job, it’s a passion. And I am so thankful I get to do something I love!” Britni and her husband Bret are parents to a very special little miracle boy named Benson who is already a singing star in their house.

Kayla Hamblen

Kayla Hamblen comes to Seedling with a wealth of theatre experience from both the teaching and performing perspectives. Kayla graduated from the University of Northern Colorado with a BA in Theatre Arts Education and has worked as a children’s theatre teacher and director in both Colorado and Las Vegas. She has an impressive list of performance roles in musicals, including the lead roles in Mary Poppins, White Christmas, Hairspray, The Wizard of Oz, and Leader of the Pack. She has been a featured soloist in many other productions and musical revues. Kayla and her husband are Colorado natives and keep busy with their cute baby girl.

Julie Johnson

Julie Johnson is a devoted theatre teacher for kids who is passionate about sharing her love of the stage with the next generation. With her years of experience and natural talent for teaching, she creates a welcoming and encouraging environment for her students to explore their creativity and hone their acting skills. Julie's infectious energy and enthusiasm make every class a fun and engaging experience, inspiring her young performers to discover their full potential. Currently, she is working on her latest production, carefully selecting the perfect script and casting the ideal actors for each role. With every rehearsal, Julie is amazed at the incredible progress her students make and the unique personalities they bring to their characters. Her ultimate goal is to create a memorable and entertaining show that will captivate and delight audiences of all ages, while instilling a lifelong love of theatre in her students. Julie can't wait to see the magic her young performers will bring to the stage and the joy they will bring to the hearts of those who watch.

Karen Gubler

Karen Gubler is a passionate theatre teacher for kids who loves to inspire and nurture the budding talent of her young performers. With her infectious energy and enthusiasm, she creates a fun and creative learning environment where children can explore their love for acting and drama. Karen is currently preparing for her next production, where she hopes to showcase the incredible skills and unique personalities of her students. Her goal is to create a memorable and enjoyable experience for both the young actors and the audience, and she can't wait to see the magic they will create together on stage.
